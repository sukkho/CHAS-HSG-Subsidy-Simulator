What This Application Does:
This application simulates a clinic visit and compares patient out-of-pocket costs under two subsidy schemes: CHAS Classic and Healthier SG Chronic Tier.

Users can input:
- CHAS card type
- Visit type (acute, simple chronic, complex chronic)
- Remaining annual subsidy balances
- Prescribed medications and quantities

The system then:
1. Calculates consultation fees and medication costs
2. Applies GST (9%)
3. Enforces per-visit and annual subsidy caps
4. Applies whitelisted drug pricing rules
5. Applies Healthier SG SDL percentage subsidies where applicable
6. Computes total subsidy and final patient payable amount

A side-by-side financial breakdown is displayed, and the application identifies which scheme results in a lower out-of-pocket payment.
